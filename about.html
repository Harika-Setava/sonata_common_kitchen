ABOUT

A common kitchen represents a commercial kitchen used solely to prepare food for delivery. These kitchens are often located in remote or densely populated areas and typically do not have any dine-in or take-out options. Instead, they focus on delivering fresh, made-to-order meals to customers promptly.
