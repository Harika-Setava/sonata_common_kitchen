Nawabi mixed vegetable curry

PREPARATION TIME: 10 MINUTES | COOKING TIME: 20 MINUTES | SERVINGS: 2


Ingredients

Potato-1
Carrot-1
Capsicum-1
Onion-1
Tomatoes-2
Beans-10
Bay Leaf-1
Cinnamon- 1 inch stick
Green Chilli-1
Red Chilli Powder- ½ tsp
Turmeric Powder- ½ tsp
Coriander Seed Powder- ½ tsp
Garam Masala Powder- ½ tsp
Salt- To Taste
Oil- 3tsp
Coriander Leaves- a few

To Grind To a Paste
Cashew Nuts- 10
Tomato- 1
Onion-1
Ginger- 1 inch bit
Garlic – 2 pods
Cumin- 1 tsp

Steps

  1. Blanch the cashew nuts in hot water. Chop onion and tomato. Chop the vegetables finely. Grind the blanched cashew nuts, onion and tomato along with ginger, garlic and cumin. Set it aside for after use. 
  2. Connect the power cord to ROBOCOOK and switch ‘ON’ button. Press the SautéÃÅ Button in front dashboard and wait for 30 secs for the inner cooking pot to get heated up.
  3. Add oil, bay leaf, cinnamon stick, green chilli, onion and tomatoes. Once it is brown add garam masala powder, turmeric powder, red chilli powder and coriander seed powder. Add now chopped vegetables like potato, beans carrot and capsicum.
  4. Add salt and two cups of water.
  5. Mix well. Add the ground paste and mix once again.
  6. Place the closing lid over the top, Turn and place the Pointer in ‘Close’ Position. Ensure the lid is firmly closed and Pressure release button should be on ‘DOWN’ Position.
  7. Press the ‘Idly ’pre-set menu button in the ROBOCOOK and select Medium.. PIC TECHNOLOGY pre-sets temperature/pressure/timer for pre-cooking
  8. After Pre-Cooking is done the cooking countdown will appear and starts down from 15 mins. Once the countdown comes down and cooking is completed it stops with 3 Beep sound and automatically switches to KEEP WARMmode. Press ‘Cancel’ if not required.
  9. Wait for 10 mins to get Natural Pressure Release (OR) it can be released manually by pulling ‘UP’ the pressure release button. Ensure the pressure is released completely on both the methods
  10. Now TWIST and Open the cooker. Switch to Keep warm or Off mode. Give a quick stir and garnish with  coriander leaves and revel in the appeasing  Nawabi Mixed Vegatable Curry.
